Let there be n7 degree 7 vertices, and n8 degree 8 vertices.

We show that n7 = 2s.

Since every edge is degree 7 or 8, the only way to have 140 edges is to have all vertices of degree 8.

If G has fewer than 140 edges, some of these edges must be missing, and every missing edge is incident to two vertices, so n7 = 2s.
